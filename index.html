Animal Name : ELEPHANT
Elephants are the world's largest land mammals, known for their intelligence, social nature, and distinctive features like large ears, thick skin, and a versatile trunk. They use their powerful trunks to drink, eat, and communicate, and they live in herds, forming strong family bonds.

Cats are domesticated carnivorous mammals from the Felidae family, known for their excellent jumping abilities and tendency to sleep for many hours each day. They have soft fur, sharp claws, and are valued as companions, having lived with humans for thousands of years.
